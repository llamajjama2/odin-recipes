Crinkle Bliss Cookies

Crinkle Bliss Cookies are a perfect blend of soft and chewy with a delightful hint of crispness on the outside. Made with rich cocoa and a touch of vanilla, these cookies offer a sweet, melt-in-your-mouth experience with every bite. As they bake, they develop a beautiful crinkled top, dusted lightly with powdered sugar, giving them their signature look and an extra touch of sweetness. Whether you're sharing them with friends or enjoying them solo with a cup of coffee, these cookies bring a comforting, blissful moment to any occasion.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Cocoa powder
  • Truffle Oil
  • Powdered sugar
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven and prepare the baking sheet
  2. Mix the dry ingredients
  3. Prepare the dough
  4. Form the cookies and bake
